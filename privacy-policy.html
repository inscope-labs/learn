Inscope: PrepWise — Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 15, 2025

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Summary: PrepWise is an educational quiz app developed by Inscope. We only collect the user’s email/username plus ephemeral analytics used to improve the app. Analytics data is collected only during runtime and deleted after the session. Users can opt out of analytics collection at any time.

1. Information we collect

PrepWise collects the minimum information required to provide and improve the app:

  • Email / Username: Collected when a user creates an account or signs in. Used only for identification, optional personalization, and account recovery.
  • Runtime analytics (ephemeral): Non-identifiable metrics collected during app sessions — examples include question performance (correct/incorrect), time spent per question, app errors/crashes, device model and OS version, and session timestamps. These analytics are used solely for product improvement and educational insights and are deleted after the session ends.

We do not request or store payment data, contacts, location, camera or microphone data. The only user-identifying field is email/username.

2. How we use information

  • App functionality: Email/username allow account access and progress tracking within a session.
  • Product improvement: Ephemeral analytics help us tune question difficulty, fix bugs, and improve learning outcomes.
  • Support: If you contact support, we may use your email to address your request.

3. Data retention & ephemeral policy

All analytics data collected for product improvement is ephemeral: it is collected only while the app runs and removed (deleted) when the session ends. PrepWise does not retain session analytics permanently.

Stored account identifiers (email/username) are kept only as long as needed to provide account functionality. If you request account deletion (see Contact & Deletion), your account identifier will be removed.

4. Third-party services

We use the following trusted service providers to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): used for secure transport, optional authentication services, and transient analytics buffering. When Firebase services are used, they act as processors on our behalf. We only share the minimum necessary runtime analytics with Firebase for processing, and those analytics are deleted according to our ephemeral policy.

We do not sell or trade personal information to advertisers or third parties.

5. Opting out of analytics

You can opt out of analytics collection at any time:

  • In-app: Open PrepWise → Settings → Privacy → toggle Analytics OFF. The app will stop collecting runtime analytics when that toggle is off.
  • Account deletion / data requests: Email afterdawn.service@gmail.com with the subject Data Deletion and we will process your request within 30 days.

If you opt out mid-session, any ephemeral analytics already collected during that session will be deleted immediately and no further analytics will be captured.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data in transit and in processing. Communications between the app and our services use encrypted channels (HTTPS / TLS). Our systems and vendor services (e.g., Firebase) employ industry standard safeguards.

No system is completely secure; if a security incident affects personal information, we will notify affected users and authorities as required by law.

7. Children

PrepWise is an educational app designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 provided us with information, please contact afterdawn.service@gmail.com to request removal.

8. Your rights

You may:

  • Request access to or deletion of your account identifier (email/username).
  • Opt out of analytics collection at any time (see Opting out).
  • Contact us with questions, corrections or privacy concerns.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Material changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact & deletion requests

For privacy questions, opt-out requests, or account deletion:
